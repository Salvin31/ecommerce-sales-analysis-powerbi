# E-Commerce Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## Overview

This project presents an interactive **E-Commerce Sales Dashboard** developed in **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer spending patterns, product categories, and regional sales trends.

The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations and Key Performance Indicators (KPIs), enabling data-driven decision-making.

## Problem Statement

Businesses generate large volumes of sales data across multiple products, customers, and geographical regions. The objective of this dashboard is to provide decision-makers with a centralized view of key business metrics and enable them to monitor sales performance, identify profitable product categories, understand customer purchasing behavior, and evaluate regional sales distribution.

---

## Dataset

The dashboard is built using an E-Commerce sales dataset containing transactional information, including:

- Orders
- Customers
- Product Categories
- Sub-Categories
- States
- Sales
- Profit
- Quantity Sold
- Payment Mode

## Dashboard Features

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard highlights the following business metrics:

- Total Sales
- Quantity Sold
- Total Profit
- Average Order Value

---

### Profit by Month

Displays monthly profit trends, helping identify seasonal business performance and fluctuations throughout the year.

---

### Payment Mode Distribution

Visualizes customer payment preferences across different payment methods.

---

### Sales by State

Compares sales performance across states to identify high-performing and underperforming regions.

---

### Customer Spending Analysis

Analyzes spending patterns across individual customers and product categories.

---

### Profit by Sub-Category

Displays profitability by product sub-category, enabling identification of the most profitable product lines.

---

## Interactive Features

The dashboard includes interactive slicers allowing users to filter the report by:

- Quarter
- Product Category

All visuals update dynamically based on user selections.
